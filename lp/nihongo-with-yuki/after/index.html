講師について / Meet Yuki

埼玉で6年間小学校教師をしていました。今は日本に住む外国人に日本語を教えています。JLPT N5〜N2対応。

I spent 6 years teaching elementary school in Saitama. The thing about teaching 8-year-olds is: if your explanation isn't clear, they'll tell you — loudly. That experience taught me how to break down grammar, vocabulary, and kanji into pieces that actually stick.

Now I teach Japanese to foreigners living in Japan. Most of my students are English speakers in their 20s–40s who need Japanese for work, daily life, or JLPT prep. I cover N5 through N2.

レッスンの中身 / What a lesson looks like

01

We find your level — precisely / レベルを正確に把握

体験レッスンで文法・読解・会話の現在地を診断します。

In the trial lesson, I assess where you are: what grammar you know, where your reading breaks down, and what's holding your speaking back. No guesswork.

02

Lessons built around your weak points / 弱点に集中

苦手な部分を重点的に練習。助詞、リスニング、アウトプットなど、一人ひとりに合わせます。

Each session targets the gaps I found. If your particle usage is shaky, we drill particles. If you can read but can't produce sentences, we do output practice.

03

Feedback sheet after every lesson / 毎回フィードバックシート

レッスン後24時間以内に、学習内容・ミス・復習ポイントをまとめたシートをお送りします。

Within 24 hours, you get a written sheet: what we covered, mistakes you made, corrections, and what to review before next time. You never lose track of progress.

よくある質問 / FAQ

Do I need any prior knowledge of Japanese?

No. About 40% of my students start at zero. We begin with hiragana and basic greetings, then build from there.

What platform do you use?

Zoom. I share my screen for grammar explanations and use a digital whiteboard for kanji practice.

Can I reschedule?

Yes, with 24 hours' notice. I'll find another slot that week.

How do I pay?

Bank transfer or PayPal. Payment details are sent after your trial lesson.
